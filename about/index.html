Who am I?


Hello. I am Gabriel Asís, an artist and Philosophy graduate (UBA) from Argentina, now based in Barcelona. Welcome to my portfolio. Some people call me Scooby because I am a fan of Buffy the Vampire Slayer. While my philosophical school of thought is bestial poptimism, my artistic style remains whimsigothic at heart.

I love blending the academic with the artistic. My final thesis assesses affectivity in knowledge construction and the aesthetics of science through a Harawayan lens. Art↔science relationalities[1] are a plentiful source of inspiration. I intend to further explore all these intersectional paths of oxymorons and would like to pursue doctoral studies on the relationship between dreams and creativity... For a comprehensive overview of my contributions in academia, visit my ORCID profile.

Let's get in touch. You can message me at: scoobygangster@gmail.com


[1] See Perriquet, O., & Seaman, B. (2011). Art↔Science Relationalities. In International Symposium on Electronic Arts, Istanbul, Turkey.
